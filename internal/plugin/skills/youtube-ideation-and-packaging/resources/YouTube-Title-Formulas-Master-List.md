## Formula: Personal Story + Revelation

**Original:** I returned a Nazi-looted painting after 80 years… here's what the Jewish family said when they saw it

**Template:** I [Did Something Significant/Unusual] after [Unexpected Length of Time or Circumstance]… here's what [Relevant Person/Group] said when [Key Moment Happened]

**Examples:**

- I apologised to my childhood bully after 30 years… here's what he said to me
- I gave my ex the wedding ring back… here's how she reacted
- I finally opened the letter my dad wrote before he died… this is what it said
- I tracked down the stranger who saved my life… here's what happened next
- I told my mum the secret I'd kept for 20 years… this was her response
- I found my biological father through a DNA test… here's what he said when I called

## Formula: Long-Term Experience + Hidden Insight

**Original:** I've driven electric vehicles for 6 years, here's one hidden thing nobody talks about.

**Template:** I've [been doing X] for [a long time], here's one [hidden/surprising/overlooked] thing nobody talks about.

**Examples:**

- I've been on a keto diet for 3 years, here's one side effect nobody talks about.
- I've worked from home for a decade, here's one challenge nobody talks about.
- I've trained hundreds of clients, here's one pattern nobody talks about.
